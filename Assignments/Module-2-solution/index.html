Learning Streams

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is a science like mathematics or biology. It studies ways to build intelligent programs and machines that can creatively solve problems,Where the Human interface is not needed,which has always been considered a human prerogative.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence (AI) that provides systems the ability to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. In ML, there are different algorithms that help to solve problems.

Deep Learning

Deep learning, or deep neural learning, is a subset of machine learning, which uses the neural networks to analyze different factors with a structure that is similar to the human neural system.Behind the scenes,AI is powered by some form of deep learning.
